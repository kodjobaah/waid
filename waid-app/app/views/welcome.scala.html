Description of Mobile App Actions:

  •   Switch between front and back camera
  •   To start and stop the camera
  •   To start and stop sharing a live stream.
  •   To log out from the program

Mobile App Usage:

  • Click on to Start Sharing and to Switch Camera off and On
  • You can send an email, post on facebook or tweet on twitter using the icons on the menu bar
  • Starting and Stopping the camera has no impact on the stream
  • Once you click on this the stream is no longer active
  • If you start sharing again you will need to re-invite your viewers so that they can view your new live stream.
